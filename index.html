Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about M&P tracking and MNP courier services in Pakistan.

Where can I track my M&P courier online?

You can track your M&P parcel at mnptracking.org.pk. Enter your 10-digit consignment number to get live delivery status instantly.

What is my M&P tracking number?

Your M&P tracking number is a 10-digit consignment ID found on your physical booking receipt, SMS confirmation, or your eCommerce order dashboard under "Track Order".

How long does M&P delivery take in Pakistan?

M&P same-day delivery is available in major cities. Standard inter-city deliveries take 1–3 business days. Bulk or freight shipments may take 3–7 business days depending on weight and destination.

Can I track M&P courier by mobile number?

Direct tracking by mobile number is not available publicly, but you can track via SMS by sending your MNP tracking number to 9900. For detailed status, use MNP Tracking online.

Is mnptracking.org.pk an official M&P website?

mnptracking.org.pk is an independent third-party tracking platform built to provide a faster and mobile-friendly tracking experience. It is not officially affiliated with M&P Express Logistics Pvt Ltd.

What cities does M&P deliver to in Pakistan?

M&P delivers to 1,600+ locations across Pakistan including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta, and all Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities nationwide.
